Who Is Captain Marvel

Carol Danvers, (Kree name; Car-Ell) aka Captain Marvel, formerly Ms. Marvel, is a former American astronaut and USAF test pilot, who worked for N.A.S.A.. She became head of security at N.A.S.A., when it was infiltrated by a Kree soldier named Captain Marvel. Because of a tesseract explosion , Carol gained special powers
